## Module 4 — Mini Ritual

> **Note:** In this situation the breathing animation does not appear in the English version (no explicit exhale cue in the ritual steps). All steps use regular 3.5-second pauses.

### Clean Text
Place both feet on the floor.
Feel your grounding.
Breathe calmly in and out.
Notice the stillness.
Place one hand on your heart or belly.
Feel your breath there.
Say inwardly: "I may allow myself time."
Stay with this for three calm breaths.

## Module 6 — Going Deeper (Premium)

### Clean Text
The silence between chapters

Inner emptiness is often experienced as frightening because we live in a culture that celebrates constant stimulation. But emptiness is not a lack. It is a transition. A space between what was and what wants to come.

In Gestalt therapy, this state is described as a fertile void: a point where old patterns have dissolved and new ones aren't yet visible. Like a seed germinating beneath the ground.

Disorientation is the absence of familiar reference points. That is uncomfortable, but it is also a sign that you are growing.

The most important guidance: don't try to fill the emptiness. Hold the space open, and trust that clarity will come.
