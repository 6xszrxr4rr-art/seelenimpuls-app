## Module 4 — Mini Ritual

> **Note:** In this situation the breathing animation does not appear in the English version (no explicit exhale cue in the ritual steps). All steps use regular 3.5-second pauses.

### Clean Text
Place both feet consciously on the floor.
Feel both feet at the same time.
Breathe calmly in and out.
Place one hand on your belly.
Feel your centre.
Gather yourself there.
Say inwardly: "I remain calm in my centre."
Stay with this for three calm breaths.

## Module 6 — Going Deeper (Premium)

### Clean Text
When the mind doesn't know what the heart has long felt

Decision doubt rarely arises because there is too little information. Most of the time, we already know what feels right, but we don't trust that knowing.

Neuroscientists like António Damasio have shown that good decisions aren't made through pure reason. Emotions and body signals (somatic markers) play a central role.

The problem with wavering is the fear of making the wrong choice. Yet in most life situations, there is no objectively wrong decision. There are paths that allow for different experiences.

A question worth sitting with: which decision can I carry most easily? Which one feels quietest in my body?
